WHAT IS HERBAL MEDICINE?

Herbal medicine is the science of using herbs for preventing and treating illness, and for promoting a healthy lifestyle. It has been the primary form of medicine in the world and has been in written texts for over 5000 years. Over 75% of the world's population still use traditional healing practices, such as herbal medicine.


The effect of Herbal treatments depends upon how serious the condition is, how long the condition has existed, the dosage and how the herb is taken. Effectiveness is also measured on how diligently treatment plans are followed. Some herbs take just a few minutes such as digestive bitters, others take up to 30 minutes when soaking in the tub for a headaches or muscle pain. Others take days or months and more difficult conditions can take years. Each individual is unique and healing can vary from person to person.
